Pre-Match Configuration

Quick & Smart Match Setup

  • Custom Team & Player Management

    Set team names and number of players (e.g., 5-a-side, 11-a-side) manually.

  • Overs Customization

    Full control over the number of overs per match.

  • Toss & Decision

    Direct selection screen for deciding who bats first.

  • Last Man Standing Mode

    Special toggle for "Single Batsman Can Play Alone". The last remaining batsman can play without a non-striker.

In-Match Experience

Advanced Live Scoring Engine

  • Real-Time Dashboard

    Clear display of Runs, Wickets, Overs, CRR, Target, and Required Run Rate (RRR).

  • Granular 'Extras' Panel

    Dedicated popup for Wide (WD), No Ball (NB), Bye, Leg Bye, Penalty Runs (5, 7), and No Ball + Runs combinations.

  • Pro 'Run Out' Mechanism

    Specify exactly who is out (Striker/Non-striker) and how many runs were completed before the dismissal.

  • Undo Feature

    Instantly revert incorrect ball or run updates with a quick UNDO button.

  • End Innings

    Manually declare or end the innings whenever necessary.

Effortless Management

  • Auto-Selection Popups

  • In-Game Modifications

  • Role Swapping
